You Feel Sadder Than Usual

If you’re opening this letter lovelove ko, then I’m guessing na malungkot ikaw ngayon, and life feels a little heavier than usual. Unang-una, gusto kong sabihin na it’s okay to have days like this, love. Palagi mo akong kasama tuwing nakakaramdam ka ng lungkot. It’s okay to be tired, to be overwhelmed, or to be sad even if you can’t fully explain why. You don’t always have to be happy, strong, or okay all the time. It’s okay to feel your own emotions. Like usual, I wish na I could be there with you ngayon, I wish na makatabi kita, mahawakan ang mga kamay mo, at yakapin ka. Gusto ko nang harap-harapan kong masabi sayo na hindi mo kailangang harapin lahat ng nararamdaman mo nang mag-isa. I wish I could take away whatever is making you feel sad :< Lovelove ko kahit na malungkot ikaw, sana alam mo na proud na proud ako sayo. I know there are moments like this where you might doubt yourself or feel like you’re not doing enough, especially dahil sa situation mo diyan ngayon, but I see how hard you try. I see all your efforts, your kindness, your patience, and the way you keep going even when things are difficult. I’m proud of you, not just when you succeed, but also when you’re struggling and you choose to keep moving forward lovelove. I want you to do something small para sa sarili mo, inom ikaw ng water, eat ikaw ng foodddd. Listen to our playlistt, hug patpat, or just let yourself rest for a while. Whenever this sadness na nararamdaman mo passes, I hope you smile again knowing na I love you so much, and I’m proud of you for everything you’ve done. I hope you know na bawat pangarap ko sa future, nagsisimula at nagtatapos din sayo. Mahal na mahal kita, Chana. Higit pa sa iniisip mo.
With love, Alvin
