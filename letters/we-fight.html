We Are Not Okay

Lovelove ko if you’re opening this, then I’m guessing we had a fight, and it hurt you so much. If we did, I want you to know something first: I still love you, I know na iniisip mo ngayon na hindi kita love, na hindi tunay yung love ko for you, but I want to remind you na I do, and that my love for you is real. Even when we’re upset, even when we don’t understand each other, even when emotions are high and things feel messy, my love for you doesn’t disappear just because we’re having a difficult moment. I know our fights can feel scary; they make us overthink and wonder if things have changed between us. It makes us wonder if we’re losing the person we love. But we are just two different people with different feelings, thoughts, and ways of handling things. Sometimes we will misunderstand each other, sometimes we might say things because we’re hurt, and sometimes we might need time to calm down. But none of that changes the fact na mahal na mahal kita, and I want us to fix things together. If we’re fighting right now, I hope we both remember to be a little bit gentler with each other. Behind the frustration is still the same person who loves you and misses you every day. I’m sorry for all the times I hurt you, whether I meant to or not. I’m still learning how to love you better, and sometimes I can be a really dumb pipilapu. Despite this, I hope you know na I never want to stop trying; I never want to stop trying to become the person you deserve. I want to continue learning about you, I want to continue understanding you better. I hope you remember this too, love. You are allowed to be upset with me and still be loved. You are allowed to need time and still be loved. You are allowed to tell me you hate me and still be loved. You are allowed to tell me how you feel and still be loved. When we talk again, I don’t want us to come back and just forget what happened. I want us to sit down, talk about it, and come back stronger, like we always do. Right? I love you. Don’t let one hard argument erase all the love we’ve built over the months we’ve been learning about each other, lovelove ko. At the end of the day, it’s still you and me, and it will always be you and me. I know we’ll always find our way back to each other. I love you, lovelove ko. If you're finding it hard to love me right now because of this fight, let me carry the two of us kahit saglit lang. We'll just be angry, but I won't ever let you go.
With love, Alvin
