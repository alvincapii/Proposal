You Doubt Us

If you’re opening this, love, I’m guessing na meron ikaw naiisip na hindi maganda about us. Maybe you’re scared, maybe you’re overthinking, maybe you’re wondering if we’ll be okay, maybe you’re thinking of ending things too. First, I want you to know na naiintindihan kita sa nararamdaman mo. Kahit na hindi mo pa sabihin sakin, lagi ko iniintindi ikaw. I want to share with you something na napapanood ko sa mga IG reels. People say na relationships are not just the hard moments. They say na, we are also the laughs we share, the random conversations we have, the little things we do for each other, the times we choose to stay, and the effort we continue to give to each other. Alam mo lovelove ko, yung story natin kahit na 1 year palang, is already made up of so many small moments that prove how much we mean to each other. I know lovelove na we are not perfect, we will have misunderstandings, bad days, moments where we feel tired, exhausted even. But I don’t want you to see those moments as signs that we are breaking. Maybe this letter could be a small reminder for me too, kasi I also see those moments as signs na we are falling apart, na we are not going to be okay. The point is, me want us to see our arguments, misunderstandings, and tampuhans as a way for us to learn how to love each other better. Whenever you feel down, I want you to remember na pinili kita hindi dahil laging madali, and hindi dahil lagi tayong masaya, pinili kita because you are someone I want to keep choosing, even when things get hard, difficult, and exhausting. You are someone worth fighting for and worth choosing every single day. Lovelove ko, if you ever feel like you’re not enough for me, and I know you sometimes do, please read this again. You are someone I am grateful to have, you are someone I always want to understand, and you are someone I want to grow with. Every dream I have for the future begins and ends with you, lovelove ko. When your doubts come, I want to remind you of all the times we made each other happy, remember the moments where we felt safe with each other, and remember that we are two completely different people trying our best, not two people trying to be perfect. Araw-araw kong hinihiling na katabi mo ako, love. I want to hold your hands and give you a massive hug, especially in moments like these. I will never stop longing and yearning for you, love. We’ve made it through so many things already, and you’re still the person I want to love every single day. I love you so much, lovelove. In all of life's uncertainties, you are the only thing I am certain of.
With love, Alvin
