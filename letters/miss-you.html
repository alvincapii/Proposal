You Miss Me

If you’re opening this letter lovelove ko, then I’m guessing you miss me right now. The first thing na gusto ko sabihin sayo is that kahit na sobrang layo natin sa sandali na to, my love for you has not gone anywhere. I love you the same way I loved you last year, last month, last week, and yesterday. In fact, my love for you grows every single day. I know na there are times when the distance feels heavier than usual, especially tuwing hindi tayo okay, tuwing magka-away tayo, and tuwing malungkot tayo. Sometimes you might wish na I was beside you, holding your hand, listening to you talk about your day, or just sitting in silence with you. Sana alam mo na araw-araw ko hinihiling yung mga bagay na yun too :< When you miss me, I want you to remember all the moments we’ve shared, remember our conversations, our random jokes, the random laughs we shared while on vc, the times we made each other smile without trying, and the times we stayed despite everything na pinagdaanan natin. I want you to remember all of these things kasi I want you to realize na what we have is real, and we will overcome everything life throws at us, kahit na mahirap, kahit na malapit na tayo sumuko, kahit na pagod na pagod na tayo. I always wish na mabigay ko sayo lahat ng gusto mo, including the biggest hugs and kisses that you could ever ask for. I would tell you na everything would be okay, and tell you how proud I am of you palagi, especially on the days na na-fefeel mo na sobrang hirap ng lahat. I miss you so much too, love, kung alam mo lang. Miss na miss na kita, sobra pa sa salitang sobra. Araw-araw akong nangungulila sa presensya mo, lovelove ko.
With love, Alvin
