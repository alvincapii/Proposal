You Feel Alone

If you’re reading this love, it means the quiet got too loud again. Maybe hindi rin tayo okay ngayon. I’m sorry if you are feeling alone ngayon, maybe I was the reason why you feel alone. I wish I could just reach through these words and sit beside you lovelove ko, I want to hold your hand, and make everything feel a little less heavy. I want to remind you that you are not truly alone, even if it feels like it. Believe me, love, I know you feel na you are sooo alone ngayon, and you feel na even I am your enemy, but I am still here. I may not always be physically beside you in the exact moment you need it, but I am with you always, in every message I send, in every “are you okay” that I ask, because I genuinely care about you. I know love na loneliness can make everything feel bigger than it really is. It can make you think that nobody cares about you, that nobody understands you, that nobody notices you, but I do, I see you, I see your efforts, your softness, your strength, even when you feel like you have none left. I’m proud of you love, for staying, and for being here, still pushing through days that feel sad and alone. Kumain naba ikaw love? Nag drink ba ikaw ng water recently? Inom ka ulit kahit na kakainom mo langg. Tayo ikaw, maybe go for a walk, maybe try to tell me what you feel, o kaya maglaro tayo togetherrr. You do not have to fix everything ngayon love, even just surviving is enough, and I’m already sooo proud of you for that. If your thoughts start getting too loud, please remember na you are loved by me, but also by people in your life who may not always know how to show it properly, kahit na alam kong sobrang bobo nila minsan ba. I wish I could be there for you and just sit quietly with you until the feeling passes. I wish I could show you na you don’t have to feel pressured to talk, and that you don’t have to explain anything anymore. :< You are not alone love, I’m here, and I’m not going anywhere. Mahal na mahal kita.
With love, Alvin
